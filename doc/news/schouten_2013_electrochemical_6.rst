**Added:**

* Added digitized data for schouten_2013_electrochemical_6 (Schouten et al., 2013):
  blank cyclic voltammograms of five Cu single crystals (Cu(111), Cu(100),
  Cu(110), Cu(322), Cu(911)) in 0.1 M NaOH from figure 1.
* Added a ``convert-svg-one`` pixi task to convert and validate a single
  svgdigitizer paper folder, for quick local checks without rebuilding all
  entries. Usage: ``pixi run -e dev convert-svg-one <paper>``.
